ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Ibeh Chinaza Blessing, the founder of Nazzy Scrivens. I hold a B.Sc. degree in Entrepreneurship Studies from Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University. My passion for business fueled my entry into freelance content writing, using my skills to support businesses, solve problems, and tell compelling stories.

I am dedicated to helping businesses grow and delighting readers with content that captivates them. Creativity and brainstorming are my strengths, and I take a mature and responsible approach to every task, consistently delivering positive solutions for people and businesses through my writing.

With years of experience as a writer, I can attest that reaching this point has required unwavering consistency and dedication. However, I have many more dreams and aspirations yet to achieve. Most of my professional career has been dedicated to freelance writing and ghostwriting, working on diverse projects ranging from content writing for articles, research papers, poetry, business plans and proposals, academic writing, copywriting, and storytelling. I also specialize in gender-based writing, advocating for women and children through my work, and have contributed to numerous published novels.

In addition to my writing career, I am a Business Development Manager, leveraging my expertise to drive growth and create strategic opportunities for businesses. Beyond writing and business development, I cherish my solitude, often spending my free time surfing the internet and conducting random, incessant research. My hobbies include driving, eating, traveling, and exploring new places. I am a cheerful and welcoming person who you would love to work with!
